About Me

My Portfolio

I am a GIMM (Games, Interactive Media, & Mobile Technology) student at Boise State University. Coming from a self taught background in graphic design, at Boise State I am now studying how the human experience and it’s translated UI can fit into many different mediums whether it be an app, Augmented Reality Experience, Virtual Reality Experience, or simply an optimized website. I want to become a UX/UI or Visual Designer at a company that makes inclusive experiences and products accessible to all. My goal is to see my accessible and inclusive designs be used to make a global impact, whether through a top 10 selling video game, or through physical products.
